Player support

How can we help?

Last Colony: Decisions is an offline game. Most issues can be resolved without sharing personal information or game data.

Email support Privacy policy

When contacting support

Please include your device model, Android or iOS version, app version, selected language and the steps that led to the issue. Do not send passwords, payment information or other sensitive personal data.

Quick troubleshooting

The game does not open or shows a blank screen

  1. Close the app completely and open it again.
  2. Restart the device.
  3. Install the latest store version.
  4. If the issue remains, email support with the device and OS version.

Sound or vibration is unavailable

Check the device's silent mode, media volume, vibration and haptic settings. The game does not use the microphone and does not play audio in the background.

Progress was removed

Progress is stored only on the device. Clearing app storage, uninstalling the game or restoring the device may remove it. The developer does not have a server copy that can be restored.

Erase local game data

The game has no user account and stores no server-side data. On Android, use Settings → Apps → Last Colony: Decisions → Storage → Clear storage. On iOS, delete the app to remove its local app container. Reinstalling starts a fresh local game.
